Alvin and the Chipmunks

About Alvin and the Chipmunks

They are playing around while aboard a cruise ship, the Chipmunks and Chipettes accidentally go overboard and end up marooned in a tropical paradise.
They discover their new turf is not as deserted as it seems.

Alvin and his brother's

Alvin's Characteristics

  1. Alvin is an emotional roller coaster and leader of the group.
  2. His enthusiasm is boundless.
  3. He is somewhat greedy and selfish, but loves his brothers.
  4. He is a fearless chipmunk

Alvin's brothers characteristics

  1. Simon is the tallest and the middle child and has an IQ just north of Albert Einstein.
  2. Theodore is the youngest chipmunk. Theodore is the child of innocence. He is shy, loving, sensitive, gullible, trusting and naive.
